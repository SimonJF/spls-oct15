SPLS

The Scottish Programming Languages Seminar is an informal meeting for the discussion of any aspect of programming languages. The next SPLS will take in October (exact date TBD) at the University of Edinburgh.

Information and updates about the October edition of SPLS will be sent via the SPLS Mailing List

Please [Organisers][contact the organisers] if you wish to offer a talk, or express a preference about the exact date.

Programme

Exact programme TBD. The meeting will begin at lunch time and take place over the course of the afternoon. There will be lunch, talks, coffee breaks, and a visit to a nearby pub.

Venue and Travel

SPLS will take place at the Informatics Forum, 10 Crichton Street, Edinburgh. The entrance is opposite that of Appleton Tower; the rooms used for the seminar and lunches will be signposted.

Further information about travelling to the Informatics Forum can be found on the School of Informatics web site.

Organisers

The organisers are Simon Fowler (simon.fowler -at- ed.ac.uk), Sam Lindley (sam.lindley -at- ed.ac.uk), and James McKinna (jmckinna -at- ed.ac.uk). Please get in touch with any questions, or if you wish to offer a talk.
